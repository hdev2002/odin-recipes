Sweet and Spicy Gochujang Chicken

A flavorful Korean dish made by marinating tender chicken pieces in a combination of gochujang (a spicy fermented red pepper paste), soy sauce, honey, garlic, and other seasonings, resulting in a tantalizing blend of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors.
